About the Application

PhoneBook is a modern web application designed for efficient contact management. Our app helps you store, organize, and quickly find information about your friends, family, and colleagues.

Key features of the application:

  • Storing contact information (name, description, phone numbers, address)
  • Categorizing contacts (family, work, friends, and others)
  • Quick search across all contacts
  • Easy addition, editing, and deletion of contacts
  • Responsive design for mobile devices and tablets
